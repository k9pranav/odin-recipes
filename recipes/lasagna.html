Ingredients

  • 1 pack lasagna noodles
  • 2 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 pound chopped vegetables
  • 1 can of canned tomatoes
  • 1 can of bechamel sauce

Directions

  1. Heat up you're pan
  2. Cook the vegetables with oil
  3. Put in the canned tomatoes
  4. Spice it up!
  5. Take a backing tray
  6. Layer it first with pasta, then the ragu, and then the bechamel sauce
  7. Bake it in the oven, til cooked
