Ingredients

    1 pack lasagna noodles
    2 tablespoon vegetable oil
    2 pound chopped vegetables
    1 can of canned tomatoes
    1 can of bechamel sauce

Directions

Heat up you're pan

Cook the vegetables with oil

Put in the canned tomatoes

Spice it up!

Take a backing tray

Layer it first with pasta, then the ragu, and then the bechamel sauce

Bake it in the oven, til cooked
