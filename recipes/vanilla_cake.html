Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 2.5 teaspoon baking powder
  • 0.25 teaspoon salt
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1.5 cups caster sugar
  • 0.5 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • oil
  • Vanilla Extract

Directions

  1. Preheat oven 180*C
  3. Combine all the dry ingredients
  4. Beat the eggs
  5. Carefully start adding sugar
  6. Beat till the volume has tripled
  7. Add melted butter and milk in the wet mix
  8. Start adding the dry ingredients
  9. Put the mixrure in a sheet/baking tray
  10. Bake it for 30 minutes
