Ingredients

  • Baking Potatoes
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoon butter

Directions

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to boil. Add Potatoes
  2. Add garlic and aromatics, and simmer till Potatoes are tender
  3. Drain the Potatoes and set aside
  4. Slowly add milk and the Potatoes in a pot
  5. Start mashing
